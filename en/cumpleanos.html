Frequently asked

Before you book

How long does the party last?

The birthday area booking is 2 hours. Room hire (full or half) is 3 hours.

Can I bring my own cake?

Ordering the cake from the centre is required. We can prepare themed cakes of 12-15 servings.

What if more guests come?

In the half room the capacity is 20 people; beyond that, €7 extra per person. With more than 15 kids an extra monitor is required (€40).

Is the deposit refunded if I cancel?

The deposit is non-refundable in case of cancellation on your part.
